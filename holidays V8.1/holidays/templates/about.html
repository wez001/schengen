Travel Calculator

About / Instructions


These pages are to work out how many days traveling can be taken in Europe at any given date. There is only a 90 day allocation out of a rolling 180 days.
Please enter your travel dates and planned dates on the travel page.
On the DaysLeft page select the date you would like to take your trip and it will let you know how many days are available on that date.
It will not take into account future dates booked, to take future dates into consideration set the test date after these booked days.

This program is to only to help. It does not constitute a right of stay resulting from its calculation.
